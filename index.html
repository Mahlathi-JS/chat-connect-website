About Us

We are a company based in Cape Town called Code Genius, and it was formed in 2020. Since this global pandemic has affected almost everyone's mental health in some way or form, our aim or vision as code genius is to provide our users a platform where they can share their stories and experiences during this pandemic. It is a way for people to get stuff off their chests while also connecting with people who can relate to their issues. We are doing this by creating a safe platform, user friendly web applications for our users.
